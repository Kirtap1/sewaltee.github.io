Academic Pedigree

Our story begins in the hallowed workshops of Prague's architectural institutes, where light became our medium and innovation our language.

What began as a student-led material study in sustainable polymer applications evolved into a rigorous exploration of form and function. Each piece in our collection carries DNA from countless academic critiques and all-night prototyping sessions.

Material Alchemy

Our proprietary recycled PETG compound undergoes 27 quality checks, achieving gallery-grade finishes through student-refined techniques.

Precision Craft

Every curve is perfected through academic-grade 3D modeling, translated into reality via our fleet of industrial printers.
